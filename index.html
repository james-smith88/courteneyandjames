The Venue

The whole day will take place in the Bull Hotel, a 14th century hotel and restaurant located the village of Wrotham, Kent.

Parking

The venue has a limited number of spaces, and should be enough for day guests. However in the case of the car park being full, all guests are free to park on Bull Lane itself. We do recommend car-pooling if possible!
